# Meta-Facebook-Instagram-Ad-Performance-Dashboard-Power-BI
This Power BI dashboard analyzes Meta (Facebook &amp; Instagram) advertising performance data. The dashboard provides insights into impressions, clicks, engagement, conversions, purchase behavior, demographic performance, and budget efficiency.
# Business Objective 
The business needs a performance tracking report for advertising campaigns running on 
Facebook and Instagram. 
The report will provide visibility into campaign reach, engagement, conversions, and budget 
utilization. 
This will enable the marketing team to: 
• Identify the most effective platform (Facebook vs Instagram). 
• Track campaign ROI and optimize budget allocation. 
• Understand audience engagement patterns.
